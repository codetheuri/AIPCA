About Us

African Independent Pentecostal Church of Africa (A.I.P.C.A) is the largest indigenous Church in Africa and was founded in Kenya in the 1920s. It has a following of approximately 1.8 million members with a strong presence in Central, Nairobi, Eastern, Rift Valley, and Coast regions of Kenya. Our Church is historically associated with the struggle for the freedom of this country from the Colonial Government.
